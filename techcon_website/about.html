Our History

TechCon started in 2010 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts in California. Over the years, it has grown into a global event that brings together the brightest minds in technology.

Our Mission

TechCon 2024 is committed to advancing innovation, collaboration, and accessibility in tech. We strive to create a platform where ideas flourish and boundaries are broken.

Past Speakers

We’ve welcomed pioneers and trailblazers from across the tech world. Here are a few notable names:

  • Dr. Maya Lin – AI Ethics Advocate and researcher on machine bias.
  • Samuel Osei – Lead Developer at EcoTech, innovating in sustainable hardware.
  • Elena Rodriguez – UX designer who helped redesign education platforms during the pandemic.
